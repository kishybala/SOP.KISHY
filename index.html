Welcome to the School of Programming Kishanganj

This program is for the youth women of rural areas.

The School of Programming in Kishanganj is dedicated to providing quality education in the field of programming and computer science. Our mission is to empower students with the skills and knowledge necessary to succeed in the technology industry.

Our curriculum is designed to be both comprehensive and engaging, ensuring that students are well-prepared for the challenges of the modern tech world. We offer courses in various programming languages, software development, web development, and more.

At the School of Programming, we believe in hands-on learning and real-world experience. Our students have the opportunity to work on real projects, collaborate with industry professionals, and participate in internships that provide invaluable insights into the tech industry.

We are committed to fostering a supportive and inclusive environment where students from all backgrounds can thrive and achieve their full potential. Join us at the School of Programming and start your journey towards a successful career in technology.
